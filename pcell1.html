WHAT WE DO?

The Placement Cell of Atma Ram Sanatan Dharma College strives to bridge the gap between employers and students ever since it was established. The Placement Cell ARSD College not only provides placement and internship opportunities but also provides students with industry specific knowledge to hone their skill set and transform them into productive and efficient beings. The Placement Cell regularly conducts seminars and workshops for developing various competencies among students and giving them an insight into the corporate world. The Job & Internship Fair organised by the Placement Cell since 2019 has been a big success with students from colleges and universities across Delhi and even outside Delhi participating in huge numbers and many companies offering internships and placements in diverse sectors to the students. The Placement Cell has opened windows of opportunities for the students to be the part of the biggest names in the world of corporate such as Deloitte, KPMG, EY, Wipro, TresVista just to name a few.
